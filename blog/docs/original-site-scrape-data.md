## 1. PROFILE / COMPANY PAGE (profile.html)

### Screenshot: `original-site-profile.png`

### Company Description
We have great pleasure to introduce ourselves as Civil and Structural Consultants, known as "Vadalkar And Associates", having our establishment very conveniently located opposite Dadar Station (West). From 1994 till Mar 2006, we worked in the name of "Vartak - Vadalkar And Associates" as consulting engineers.

We have a well-experienced design team of qualified Engineers in our office. We have very senior structural engineers as our advisers. We have successfully handled many projects to the entire satisfaction of our clients. We provide training and technical support for **STAAD-Pro** software package for many consultancy firms in Mumbai and work as consultants to structural consultancy firms for their software needs.

### Activities of the Firm
We are practicing as Consulting Structural Engineers. Our scope of service includes designing, detailing, estimating and occasional site supervision for various projects like industrial buildings, commercial and residential buildings, etc. We have software dealership for STAAD/Pro family software products. We also arrange Municipal liaison works through our associates.

### Manpower Resources
Key person is a structural engineer having experience in design and site work. Our current employment is three full time engineers, two draftsmen and one office assistant. We also frequently employ external draftsmen on the job basis.

### Facilities
Fully furnished office premises admeasuring @ 300 sq. ft. at Dadar and other office at Vashi. One Telephone Answering System with facsimile and one Ammonia Blue Printing machine.

We have ten computers, two printers and one A1 size plotter with network and e-mail facilities. Most of the drafting work is done on computer and manual drafting is done partly as per the requirement.

We are using the most powerful Structural Analysis and Design Software Package **[STAAD-Pro](http://www.staadpro.com)**.

We have good library of technical journals, Engineering books and various I.S. Codes.
